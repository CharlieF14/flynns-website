This is how my project incorporates parts of the Universal Design

My project incorporates the Simple and Intuitive use segment of the Universal design by having text relating to what everything does

My project incorporates the Perceptable Information segment in a similar manner to the Simple and Intuitive use by having text labelling inputs and buttons

My project incorporates the Tolerance for Error segment by not refreshing the page after an invalid card, alowing the user to re-enter a number. It also will not be valid unless every input is filled in order to have no errors with data.

My project also incorporates the Low Physical Effort segment by having a simple layout and pleasing shapes
